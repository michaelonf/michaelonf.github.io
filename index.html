Hi. I'm Michael.

I am a PhD student on the Co-Centre for Sustainable Food Systems project at University College Dublin. My research focuses on nutrition and sustainability. I am particularly interested in healthy diets from sustainable sources, and understanding what we can do to improve nutritional sustainability at a population level. I like working with data!

Previously, I worked as a Research Assistant on the UCD PLAN'EAT Living Lab team, and I hold a BSc in Food Innovation from Technological University Dublin.
